Investment Intelligence · Multi-Strategy
Aristos Council — Multi-Agent Equity Research

A multi-strategy research council where four specialist agents — Fundamental, Technical, Sentiment, Risk — plus an adversarial Critic and a Decision agent deliberate over a stock and issue an auditable Buy/Hold/Sell verdict. Strategies are swappable: dividend-income and growth-at-a-reasonable-price (GARP) run on a shared criterion registry. Agents never do arithmetic — every figure is computed deterministically and traced to source, a provenance audit rejects any unverifiable number, and five veto triggers escalate contested calls to human review. Degrades honestly on thin, foreign, or insufficient data rather than fabricating.
